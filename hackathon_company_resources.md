## üéØ Getting Started Tips

1. **Start with Official Cookbooks** - Most companies maintain GitHub repositories with working examples
2. **Check OpenAI Cookbook** - It integrates with many third-party services and is a great reference
3. **Use Vector DB Examples** - The OpenAI Cookbook has examples for every major vector database
4. **Leverage Templates** - Vercel, Cloudflare, and Streamlit offer ready-to-deploy templates
5. **Join Communities** - Discord servers and forums often have additional resources
