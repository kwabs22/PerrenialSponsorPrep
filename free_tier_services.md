## Free Tier Gotchas

| Service | Watch Out For |
|---------|---------------|
| **Vercel** | Serverless timeout 10s (Hobby) |
| **Supabase** | Pauses after 1 week inactive |
| **Render** | Sleeps after 15 min inactive |
| **Railway** | $5 credit runs out |
| **PlanetScale** | Sleep after 7 days inactive |
| **Firebase** | Spark plan has daily limits |
| **Cloudflare R2** | No spending cap (abuse risk) |
